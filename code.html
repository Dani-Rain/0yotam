ANTMAN


The Analysis of Topic Model Network


'ANTMN' was developed by Dr. Yotam Ophir & Dr. Dror Walter to equip researchers with an inductive, data-driven approach for the identification of media frames.
(News Frame Analysis: An Inductive Mixed-Method Computational Approach. Communication Methods & Measures Walter, D., & Ophir, Y., 2019)


Media framing is the selective emphasis of specific features of events and people, at the expense of others. Originaly identified as a core journalistic practice, Frames are now understood to be lense that offers imperative insight to all forms of cumminication, from politicians to laypersons on social media
